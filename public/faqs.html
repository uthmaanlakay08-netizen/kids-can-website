Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our foundation and services.

How can I apply for financial assistance?
You can apply by visiting our "Get Help" page and filling out the application form. Our team will review your application and get in touch with you to discuss the next steps.
Where does my donation go?
Donations go directly towards providing medical treatment assistance, care packages, family support services, and educational grants for children fighting cancer. We pride ourselves on transparency and efficient use of funds.
How can I volunteer with Kids-Can?
Please fill out the contact form on our "Contact Us" page with the subject "Volunteer Interest". We regularly have opportunities for hospital visits, event support, and administrative assistance.
